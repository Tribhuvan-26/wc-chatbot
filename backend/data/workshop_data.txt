Workshop Carnival 2.0 - MLRIT-CIE

Workshop Carnival 2.0 is a technical event organized by MLRIT-CIE where students can learn, build, and compete across various modern technology domains.

--------------------------------------------------

Event Dates:

Workshop Carnival 2.0 will be conducted over 2 days.

Day 1:
Workshops and hands-on learning sessions in selected domains.

Day 2:
Continuation of workshops and a contest in the second half of the day.

--------------------------------------------------

Event Overview:

Workshop Carnival 2.0 is designed to provide hands-on experience in trending tech fields. It includes workshops, practical sessions, and competitions.

Students will gain real-world exposure and compete with others in their chosen domains.

--------------------------------------------------

Domains / Workshops:

The domains available in Workshop Carnival 2.0 are:

1. App Development
Learn how to build mobile applications, UI logic, and deployment.

2. Web Development
Learn frontend and backend development using modern technologies.

3. Agentic AI
Learn to build intelligent AI agents, automation systems, and smart tools.

4. Ethical Hacking (Cybersecurity)
Learn penetration testing, vulnerabilities, and system security.

5. Data Analytics
Learn data processing, visualization, and extracting insights.

6. UI/UX Design
Learn user interface design, user experience principles, and design thinking.

Domains are also called:
fields, tracks, areas, workshops.
